Revolutionizing how business gets done.

Commissionize is more than a sales and marketing tool, but a mobile marketplace where customers and business owners can help each other make money. This new way of doing business forms a powerful alliance between customers and business owners large and small. Commissionize creates the ultimate win/win for everyone.

  • 65% of new business comes from word of mouth
  • 90% of small businesses rely on word of mouth
  • 59% of Americans say buying decisions depend on word of mouth
  • 83% of customers are willing to refer after a positive experience

Coupons, discounts and credits are a thing of the past. People want cash. Give it to them.

Nowadays, customers are savvy enough to see through marketing tactics that benefit your business over their needs. If a customer chooses to help you sell and grow your business, why not help them improve their lives too? Offering a discount or points that can only be used at your business just doesn't seem fair.

  • If your customers want to buy something somewhere else with the cash they've earned for helping you, let them! They'll come back to you because you are now a partner who is helping them make money and prosper.

    • 98% of people surveyed said they would rather receive cash than coupons, credits or discounts
    • 53% of people surveyed said being offered coupons for their help in making sales was not a big incentive
    • 84% of people surveyed say the idea of being paid in cash for helping make sales interested them
    • 81% of people surveyed said they would be more likely to shop at a business that paid them cash for referrals, than those who did not (if quality of said business was equal)
